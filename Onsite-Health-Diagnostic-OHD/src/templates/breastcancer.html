Breast
Cancer

Breast cancer can occur in women and rarely in men. Symptoms of breast cancer include a lump in the breast, bloody discharge from the nipple and changes in the shape or texture of the nipple or breast. Its treatment depends on the stage of cancer. It may consist of chemotherapy, radiation, hormone therapy and surgery.

Symptoms

  • 1. Newly inverted nipple
  • 2. Lump in the breast
  • 3. Dimpling of skin
  • 4. Change the breast size
  • 5. Fluid comig from the nipple
  • 6. Red scaly patch of skin on the breast

Prevention

  • 1. Eat a healthy diet
  • 2. Keep a healthy weight
  • 3. Avoid smoking and drinking
  • 4. Maintain your blood sugar level
  • 5. Keeps your body physically active

Treatment

  • 1. Surgery
  • 2. Chemotherapy
  • 3. Targeted therapy
  • 4. Hormonal therapy
  • 5. Radition therapy
