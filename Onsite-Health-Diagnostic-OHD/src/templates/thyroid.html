THYROID DISEASE PREDICTION

THYROID
DISEASE

The two main types of thyroid disease are hypothyroidism and hyperthyroidism. Both conditions can be caused by other diseases that impact the way the thyroid gland works. Conditions that can cause hypothyroidism include: Thyroiditis: This condition is an inflammation (swelling) of the thyroid gland.

Symptoms

  • 1. Newly inverted nipple
  • 2. Lump in the breast
  • 3. Dimpling of skin
  • 4. Change the breast size
  • 5. Fluid comig from the nipple
  • 6. Red scaly patch of skin on the breast

Prevention

  • 1. Eat a healthy diet
  • 2. Keep a healthy weight
  • 3. Avoid smoking and drinking
  • 4. Maintain your blood sugar level
  • 5. Keeps your body physically active

Treatment

  • 1. Surgery
  • 2. Chemotherapy
  • 3. Targeted therapy
  • 4. Hormonal therapy
  • 5. Radition therapy
